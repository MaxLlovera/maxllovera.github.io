Legend of Landama (University Project)

Legend of Landama is a turn-based RPG that follows a heroine who arrives in the town of Landama to save its people from villains and monsters. Accompanied by a mage companion, she will explore the village, interact with its inhabitants, and face enemies in a dynamic turn-based combat system. As a Game Designer, I was responsible for designing the levels, puzzles, and combat system, ensuring a strategic and engaging gameplay experience. This project was developed as part of the "Project II" course at university..
